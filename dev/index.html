Pigs are one of the smartest pets

Pigs are some of the smartest animals around! Not only are they smarter than even dogs, with great memory and also abstract thinking. Being one of the most laid back animals, they make for a perfect companion to chill with! They are one of the few animals found to be able to play video games!

Pigs are the best pals to chill with!

Pigs are easy eaters and hardy animals. They don’t shed and are very clean (despite what some people may think!) They are quite easy to get along with! But there are some quirks about them that you may need to know when handling these witty companions.

And one of the most emotionally complex animals

Pigs are very social animals and can develop deep bonds with others. Hence they can be very affectionate when it comes to interactions and always love a good belly rub or a long snuggling session with their favorite other!
