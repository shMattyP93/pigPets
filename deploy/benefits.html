Why Pigs May be Your Next Best Friends

Learn about these amazing creatures!

Pigs are some of The Smartest Pets

Pigs are some of the smartest animal around! Not only are they smarter than dogs, with great memory and also abstract thinking. Being one of the most laid back animals, they make for a perfect companion to chill with! They are one of the few animals found to be able to play video games!

Their cognitive ability allows pigs to play joystick-controlled video games, have abstract thoughts, use tools, recognize their own names, dreams and follow commands.

Acording to PETA

This ability makes them one of the wittiest pets around

Don’t believe us? Explore their talents below!

    PIGS ARE WITTY TRICKSTERS

    Pigs love to play and engage in mock fighting with each other, similar to how dogs and other mammals play

    PIGS HAVE KEEN MEMORY

    Pigs have excellent long-term memories and excel at whizzes with mazes and other tests requiring location of objects.

  • +

    PIGS ARE FLUENT COMMUNICATORS

    They can comprehend a simple symbolic language and can learn complex combinations of symbols for actions and objects.

  • +

    PIGS CAN BE GREAT GAMERS

    Pigs can manipulate a joystick to move an on-screen cursor, which is a capacity they share with chimpanzees

And one of the most Emotionally complex animals!

There are some interesting studies demonstrating emotional contagion in pigs. This involves responses to other pigs’ anticipation of positive or negative events, revealing the importance of social factors in a pig emotional life.

They are also Easy-Going Pals

Pigs are smart, clean, and generally non-allergenic. They are also odor-free, flea-free, charming and inexpensive to feed. Pigs are social and bond easily with humans. They’ll readily roll over for a tummy rub, as well as snuggle with you. They don’t bark, prowl the streets or spread rabies. Compared with dog droppings, mild smelling “pig berries” are a breeze to clean up.
